The Setting

It is the 41st millenium. The whole galaxy is torn in conflict. The Imperium of Man, a shattered and fascist planet-spanning empire, stands alone against the mutant, the heretic, and the alien. New threats arise, and old dangers return. Humanity's Space Marines, genetically enhanced super-soldiers, fight against the forces of Chaos and the ever-growing tide of alien invasion. There is no peace. There is only war.
